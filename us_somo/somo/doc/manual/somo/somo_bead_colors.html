SOMO - SOMO Bead Color Coding:

Last updated: January 2021

Here we describe the SOMO color coding.

Color coding is used in SOMO Bead model files.

The color code can be any integer from 0 to 15, however certain color codes have special meaning.

In particular:

  • 0 means the bead is too small to be relevant and will be excluded from SMI hydrodynamic calculations.
  • 6 means the bead is buried and will be excluded from some hydrodynamic calculation methods.
